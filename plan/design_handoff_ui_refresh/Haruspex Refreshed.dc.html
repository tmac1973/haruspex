How does grid-scale battery storage compare to pumped hydro for renewables?

Both store energy for the grid, but they differ sharply on siting, lifespan, and round-trip efficiency:

Round-trip efficiency

Lithium-ion batteries return about 85–95% of stored energy, versus roughly 70–80% for pumped hydro [1].

Siting & scale

•Pumped hydro needs specific geography and huge upfront works [2].
•Batteries are modular and deploy almost anywhere in months.
